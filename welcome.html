INSTRUCTIONS


ALL LEVELS: Avoid enemy insects by using the arrow keys (← → ↑ ↓) to move your character around the screen. Collect gems for the following bonuses:

  • BLUE GEM: SLOWS DOWN ENEMIES
  • GREEN GEM: +1 TO SCORE
  • GOLD GEM: MAKES YOU INVINCIBLE
  • HEART: +1 TO REMAINING LIVES (MAX OF 5 LIVES)


MEDIUM LEVEL: Collect Gems. Avoid Enemies. Avoid Rocks.


HARD LEVEL: Collect Gems. Avoid Enemies. Avoid Rocks. Number of enemies increase each time you score.


CHARACTER CHOICE: Change your character any time by pressing the SPACE BAR or ENTER KEY.

Good Luck Player!
